✨ I added actual collision maps to https://t.co/q1vYLwMhcW as @javilopen kept telling me to do at our weekly coworking day today

This means now the drone can not fly through buildings but it can fly through holes inside buildings like windows etc.

Buildings are important because you can take cover there, bullets also hit the collision maps

At the end of the video to show it worked I flew the drone a bit too hard into the wall so it died 😂

The tech behind it is Mesh BVH, so cool!!!

"Mesh BVH is a THREE.js addon that builds a Bounding Volume Hierarchy from a mesh's triangles. A BVH is a tree where each node holds a box containing some triangles; child nodes split those triangles into smaller boxes. Queries walk the tree and skip entire branches that can't possibly contain the answer, turning "check all 5,000 triangles" into "check ~log₂(5000) ≈ 12 boxes."
